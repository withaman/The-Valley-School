ABOUT

THE VALLEY SCHOOL

Welcome to The Valley School. We are a school which specializes in all boys’ boarding education for pupils aged 12-18. The school is probably the only ‘All India’ school with applications from almost every state each year, as well as from Indian families overseas. Established in 1935, The Valley School is one of India’s finest schools, with a strong intellectual heartbeat.The Valley School is a full boarding school for boys only and not simply a school which welcomes boarders. The school’s beautiful seventy acre campus has a vast range of flora, fauna and bird life.
